Meet Dr. Voorhees


Mr. Bob and Me

Experienced care

Dr. Voorhees has many years' experience working in small animal private practice.  After graduating from Tufts in 2006, she worked for a year on the North Shore of Boston prior to moving to North Carolina in 2008 to be closer to family.  She lives near the Pinehurst golf resorts and enjoys spending free time with her husband and mini Australian Shepherd, and a noisy cockatiel.

Tufts University

Houghton College

Services

Relief you can count on

Preventative Care & Internal Medicine 

My primary focus and passion is to help people better care for their animals and ensure our customers get the best possible care while in your clinic. With this in mind, I am pleased to provide the following services:

  • Canine and feline medicine and wellness exams
  • Exotics care for pocket pets and maintenance avian procedures (i.e., nail and wing trims)
  • Excellent client education and customer service

Surgery Policy

Before scheduling surgeries, I must establish a working relationship with your staff and have familiarity with clinic protocols and equipment.  

In general, I am willing to offer the following surgical services:

  • Canine and feline spays and neuters (prefer canine <35 pounds)
  • Minor dental procedures 
  • Will not perform ear croppings, tattooing, or orthopedic surgery
  • Other surgical services on a case-by-case basis
